Yusmarg:
- Brief Info: Tranquil destination in the Badgam district, known as the "Meadow of Jesus."
- Special Features: Lush meadows, pine forests, Doodhganga River.
- Activities: Trekking, horse riding.
- Seasonal Attractions: Colorful flowers in spring and summer, peaceful environment.
- How to Reach: 50 km from Srinagar, easily reachable by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry.

Pahalgam:
- Brief Info: Picturesque town in Anantnag district, gateway to Amarnath Yatra.
- Special Features: Lidder River, Betaab Valley.
- Activities: Trout fishing, river rafting, golfing.
- Seasonal Attractions: Lush meadows, scenic beauty, tranquil retreat.
- How to Reach: 95 km from Srinagar, accessible by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry.
View on Google Maps

Sonamarg:
- Brief Info: Valley in Ganderbal district known as "Meadow of Gold."
- Special Features: Sindh River, Thajiwas Glacier.
- Activities: Trekking, glacier visits.
- Seasonal Attractions: Snow-covered peaks, golden meadows, high-altitude lakes.
- How to Reach: 80 km from Srinagar, accessible by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry.
View on Google Maps

Doodhpathri:
- Brief Info: Unspoiled meadow in Budgam district, "Valley of Milk."
- Special Features: Dense pine and deodar forests, milky appearance of the water.
- Activities: Picnics, nature walks, short treks.
- Seasonal Attractions: Pristine environment, fresh mountain air.
- How to Reach: 42 km from Srinagar, well connected by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry.
View on Google Maps

Sinthan Top:
- Brief Info: High-altitude mountain pass at the border of Kashmir and Jammu.
- Special Features: Panoramic views, snow-covered terrain.
- Activities: Snow skiing, snowboarding.
- Seasonal Attractions: Snow activities, adventurous drive.
- How to Reach: 130 km from Srinagar, accessible by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry.
View on Google Maps

Tulip Garden:
- Brief Info: Asia's largest tulip garden in Srinagar, officially known as Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip Garden.
- Special Features: Over a million tulips, vibrant colors.
- Activities: Viewing tulip beds, Tulip Festival.
- Seasonal Attractions: Spring blooms, scenic backdrop of snow-capped mountains.
- How to Reach: 9 km from Srinagar city center, accessible by road.
- Entry Ticket: INR 50 for adults, INR 25 for children.
View on Google Maps

Dal Lake:
- Brief Info: Iconic lake in Srinagar, known as the "Jewel in the crown of Kashmir."
- Special Features: Houseboats, shikaras, floating gardens.
- Activities: Shikara rides, water sports, birdwatching.
- Seasonal Attractions: Floating market, Mughal gardens along the lake.
- How to Reach: Centrally located in Srinagar, easily accessible by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry, Shikara ride charges apply.
View on Google Maps

Shankaracharya Temple:
- Brief Info: Ancient temple on Shankaracharya Hill in Srinagar, dedicated to Lord Shiva.
- Special Features: Panoramic views of Srinagar and Dal Lake.
- Activities: Pilgrimage, scenic views.
- Seasonal Attractions: Spiritual experience, serene environment.
- How to Reach: 5 km from Srinagar city center, accessible by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry.
View on Google Maps

2. Jammu

Vaishno Devi Temple:
- Brief Info: Famous Hindu pilgrimage site near Katra, dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi.
- Special Features: Located at 5,200 feet altitude, involves a 13-kilometer trek.
- Activities: Trekking, pony rides, helicopter rides.
- Seasonal Attractions: Pilgrimage experience, natural beauty.
- How to Reach: 13 km trek from Katra, helicopter and pony services available.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry, helicopter ride charges apply.
View on Google Maps

Raghunath Temple:
- Brief Info: One of the largest temple complexes in northern India, dedicated to Lord Rama.
- Special Features: Seven shrines, gold-plated spires, intricate carvings.
- Activities: Religious rituals, festivals.
- Seasonal Attractions: Peaceful environment, cultural events.
- How to Reach: Located in Jammu city, easily accessible by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry.
View on Google Maps

Mansar and Surinsar Lakes:
- Brief Info: Twin lakes near Jammu, sacred and culturally significant.
- Special Features: Lush green hills, temples, migratory birds.
- Activities: Picnics, boating, nature photography.
- Seasonal Attractions: Scenic beauty, tranquil ambiance.
- How to Reach: 60 km from Jammu city, accessible by road.
- Entry Ticket: Free entry.
View on Google Maps
