LADAKH

LADAKH: Ladakh, located in the northernmost region of India, is known for its stunning landscapes, including vast deserts, rugged mountains, and serene lakes. It is a high-altitude desert situated in the Himalayas, characterized by its unique culture, influenced by Tibetan Buddhism. Ladakh's capital, Leh, serves as a gateway for exploring the region's monasteries, trekking routes, and natural beauty. The area is also famous for its vibrant festivals, traditional crafts, and the warm hospitality of its people. Ladakh's challenging climate, with extremely cold winters and pleasant summers, adds to its allure for adventure seekers and nature lovers.
